The man who tricked the death

Sisyphus was a smart king of the city Corinth,
he was very interested in the matters of the gods.
Some day he saw Aegena, the daughter of Asopus a river god, being kidnapped
by Zeus in the form of an eagle.
He quickly realized he could use this information to help his kingdom, which struggled with
drinking water.
He went to the river god who was afraid of his daughters disappearence and
told him he knew where his daughter was, but the information comes with
a price.
The river god agreed and created a spring of pure water for Sisyphus's kingdom and the king
told him what he has seen.
After Zeus heard of what Sisyphus has done, he hired Thanatos (or Death) to take Sisyphus's life.
When Thanatos arrived, Sisyphus had already a plan to escape him.
The king complimented Thanatos and said he had never seen such a beautiful god.
He asked Death to come in his palace and gifted him beautiful jewelry,
but when Thanatos took on his new ornaments, he realized he had been tricked.
The pretty necklace and bracelets were all chained and before he could try to escape,
it was already too late and Death got trapped.
Time passed and no one died, the realm of undead didn't get any new servents and the
god of war Ares got bored of the all the pointless wars he set because no one died.
Ares became very unpleasured and travelled to Corinth to free Thanatos.
He broke through the palace and unchained Death.
When he was finally freed, Thanatos's first target was Sisyphus the man who had chained him.
But the smart king had a plan, he told his wife not to make any kind funeral and wait for him to come back.
When Thantos arrived, he took Sisyphus's life without the king resisting.
Sisyphus arrived in the underworld and appologized for what he had done to Hades, the king of the underworld
He said: "If I had known I would cause harm to the great god of the underworld,
I never would have done so.
Aware that I am in dept to you, I have a please.
My ignorant wife denied to perform a funeral for me.
May I go back to her, take revenge and make a proper funeral..."
Hades agreed and said he has the permission to go back to the living for one day.
When Sisyphus arrived in his palace, he packed his stuff and went far away with his
wife, he lived a long and good life.
But at some point Thanatos needed to come again and this time
the kings intelligence couldn't safe him.
He arrived again in the underworld and was sent to Tartarus where he should
roll a big rock up a mountain, but every time he nearly reaches the summit,
the rock becomes immeasurably heavy and rolled down again.
